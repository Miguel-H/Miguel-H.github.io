About me

Hello, my name is Miguel Hernandez. I am a student at Canoga Park High School, and a second year mentee in the Northrup Grumman HIP Program. My goal after High School is to pursue a career in electrical or computer engineering. The reason I have chosen to pursue a career in the field of engineering is because I enjoy challenging my mind and solving problems. However, a career in science or mathematics wasn't the first option strirring in my head. Originally, I wanted to work in animation and contribute to our next generations of animated shows and films. But when utilized my creativity in designing and programming robots, I skewed towards a career in engineering. The experience of working with the LEGO Mindstorms robotics kits to design robots that complete certain challenges caught my interest, so I looked into other programs that would offer the same experience. I joined the HIP program so I can work in and get an impression of the engineering experience. My expectations after I have finished the program is that I would have gained some of the skills and preparation I will need in pursuing my career.
